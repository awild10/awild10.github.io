What are your prices?

$35 for a half hour, $52.50 for 45 minutes, $70 for an hour.

Where do lessons take place?

I offer both in-home and online video lessons. Learn in the comfort of your own home!

What do you teach?

I teach well rounded music lessons for all levels of guitar and beginner through intermediate levels for piano and ukulele. I also teach skills like composition, improvisation and music theory, either as a regular part of instrumental lessons, or as standalone instruction.

Can you teach ____ style/genre?

Generally yes, however if you're an experienced intermediate or advanced student there may be certain styles I won't be a good fit for, depending on your goals. If this is you, please communicate what you're looking for through the contact form and I'll see if we're a good fit! I'm most knowledgeable and experienced teaching rock, pop, classical and music from media (video games, movies etc).

What is your cancellation policy?

I need 24 hour notice for any cancellations! For withdrawals from lessons I need a weeks’ notice. The notice for withdrawals doesn’t apply for the first 4 lessons.

How does billing work?

Billing is all through Square! You receive an email or text and pay with a card.

Do you teach from a book? Will we read music?

I do, but both are totally optional based on your preferences! My recommended approach is using a method book for consistent linear progress, while also working together to fill out your ‘song wishlist’ and learn those songs as you’re ready.
